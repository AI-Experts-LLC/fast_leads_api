## âœ… Quality Checklist

Before sending any email, verify:
- [ ] **Length**: 75-100 words
- [ ] **Subject line**: 30-50 characters
- [ ] **Opening**: 1 sentence of genuine personalization
- [ ] **Value prop**: Mentions Energy-as-a-Service, no upfront capital
- [ ] **CTA**: Soft question, not meeting request
- [ ] **Tone**: Conversational ("Hey" not "Dear," contractions)
- [ ] **Mobile-friendly**: Short paragraphs, scannable
- [ ] **No AI tells**: Zero m-dashes, no perfect grammar
- [ ] **No links**: Email body should have no hyperlinks
